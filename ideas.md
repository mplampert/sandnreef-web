## Idea 1: "Nautical Industrial" — Rugged Maritime Workmanship

**Design Movement**: Industrial Marine meets New England Coastal — inspired by boatyard signage, naval engineering diagrams, and weathered dock aesthetics.

**Core Principles**:
1. Raw authenticity — textures that feel like salt-worn wood and brushed steel
2. Bold, no-nonsense typography that commands attention like hull lettering
3. Asymmetric layouts that mirror the organized chaos of a working marina
4. Dark, moody atmosphere with strategic pops of signal colors

**Color Philosophy**: Deep charcoal (#1a1a2e) as the dominant tone — representing the depth of the ocean at dusk. Paired with oxidized copper (#c17f59) for warmth and trust, bright signal white (#f0f0f0) for clarity, and the brand teal (#007780) used sparingly as an accent to tie back to the existing identity.

**Layout Paradigm**: Full-bleed sections with staggered content blocks. Hero uses a split-screen approach — video on one side, bold text on the other. Service cards use an overlapping masonry-style grid. Content breaks are achieved with diagonal clip-paths mimicking wave angles.

**Signature Elements**:
1. Thin horizontal rules with small anchor/compass icons as section dividers
2. Oversized service numbers (01, 02, 03) in a stencil font behind content blocks
3. Subtle grain/noise texture overlay across dark sections

**Interaction Philosophy**: Deliberate, mechanical transitions — elements slide in from the sides like dock equipment. Hover states reveal additional detail with a "flip panel" effect on service cards.

**Animation**: Scroll-triggered parallax on the hero video. Service cards stagger-fade from left to right. CTA buttons have a subtle "engine rumble" micro-vibration on hover. Section transitions use a horizontal wipe effect.

**Typography System**: Display — "Oswald" (bold, condensed, uppercase for headings). Body — "Source Sans 3" (clean, highly readable). Accent — monospaced "JetBrains Mono" for phone numbers and technical details.

## Idea 2: "Cape Cod Editorial" — Clean Coastal Storytelling

**Design Movement**: Editorial design meets coastal lifestyle — inspired by premium sailing magazines, Cape Cod travel guides, and luxury marine brand catalogs.

**Core Principles**:
1. Generous whitespace as a design element — let the content breathe like ocean air
2. Photography-forward layout where images do the heavy lifting
3. Refined serif/sans-serif pairing that feels both classic and contemporary
4. Horizontal rhythm and reading flow that guides the eye naturally

**Color Philosophy**: Warm sand (#f5f0e8) as the base — evoking the beaches of Mashpee. Deep navy (#0c2340) for authority and trust. The brand teal (#007780) as the primary action color. Soft driftwood gray (#a8a29e) for secondary elements. Coral (#e07a5f) as a rare highlight for urgency/CTAs.

**Layout Paradigm**: Magazine-style grid with alternating full-width and split sections. Hero is a dramatic full-viewport video with minimal overlay text. Services presented in an editorial two-column layout with large photography on one side and text on the other, alternating sides. Footer uses a wide three-column layout.

**Signature Elements**:
1. Thin elegant underlines that animate on scroll beneath section headings
2. Pull-quote style callouts for key selling points, set in italic serif
3. Subtle wave-shaped SVG dividers between sections in sand/teal tones

**Interaction Philosophy**: Smooth, unhurried transitions that feel like a gentle tide. Content reveals on scroll with fade-up animations. Hover states are subtle — opacity shifts and gentle scale transforms.

**Animation**: Smooth fade-up reveals on scroll (staggered 100ms between elements). Hero text fades in with a slight upward drift. Navigation has a backdrop-blur glass effect on scroll. Service images have a slow Ken Burns zoom on hover.

**Typography System**: Display — "DM Serif Display" (elegant, editorial headings). Body — "DM Sans" (clean, modern, excellent readability). The contrast between serif headings and sans-serif body creates a premium editorial feel.

## Idea 3: "Tidal Brutalist" — Bold Marine Minimalism

**Design Movement**: Neo-brutalism meets ocean — inspired by maritime safety signage, tide charts, and the raw power of the Atlantic coast.

**Core Principles**:
1. Extreme typographic scale — massive headings that demand attention
2. Hard geometric shapes with thick borders, no rounded corners
3. High contrast color blocks used as structural elements
4. Function-first layout where every element earns its space

**Color Philosophy**: Pure white (#ffffff) and jet black (#0a0a0a) as the structural foundation. Brand teal (#007780) used in large, bold blocks — not as an accent but as a statement. Bright safety yellow (#f0c808) for critical CTAs and alerts. Raw concrete gray (#d4d0c8) for texture sections.

**Layout Paradigm**: Rigid grid with thick visible borders between sections. Hero is a full-width block with enormous text overlaying the video. Services displayed in a bento-box grid with thick 4px borders. Each section is a distinct "tile" with its own background treatment. No gradients, no blur — everything is sharp and intentional.

**Signature Elements**:
1. Thick black borders (4px+) around content blocks, creating a "tile" effect
2. Rotated text labels on section edges (like maritime chart annotations)
3. Bold geometric shapes (circles, triangles) used as decorative markers

**Interaction Philosophy**: Snappy, immediate responses. No easing curves — elements appear instantly or with sharp linear transitions. Hover states use bold color inversions (teal bg → white text becomes white bg → teal text).

**Animation**: Elements snap into view on scroll with no easing. Hover triggers instant color block inversions. CTA buttons shift position slightly on hover (2px translate). Page sections have hard horizontal line reveals.

**Typography System**: Display — "Space Grotesk" (geometric, bold, modern). Body — "Space Grotesk" at lighter weights for consistency. Numbers use tabular figures for alignment in service listings.
